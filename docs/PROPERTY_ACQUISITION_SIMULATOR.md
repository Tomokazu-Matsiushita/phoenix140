# Sprint 3.3 Property Acquisition Simulator

## Purpose

Sprint 3.3 adds a new property acquisition simulator.

It is designed to evaluate a new apartment purchase before committing capital.

## New Page

```text
Property Acquisition Simulator
```

## Inputs

- purchase price
- loan amount
- interest rate
- loan term
- acquisition cost rate
- renovation cost
- initial cash buffer
- full monthly rent
- vacancy rate
- fixed asset tax
- management fee
- cleaning fee
- insurance
- repair reserve
- other annual expense

## Outputs

- monthly payment
- annual debt service
- full annual rent
- effective annual rent
- operating expense
- NOI
- cash flow after debt
- DSCR
- gross yield
- effective gross yield
- NOI yield
- cash-on-cash return
- break-even occupancy
- payback years
- CFO judgement

## Default Scenario

The default values are based on the oblige新田町 acquisition plan.

These are planning defaults and should be verified before any investment decision.
